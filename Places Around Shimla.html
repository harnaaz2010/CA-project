1.Kufri


Kufri is known for its tranquil surroundings, breathtaking views, and stunning locales, making it one of the best places to visit in Shimla. Often referred to as the Switzerland of the East, Kufri's mesmerizing beauty attracts tourists throughout the year.

Duration: 5-6 hrs
Ideal Visit Time: April-June

2.Hatu Peak


The Hatu Peak is considered to be a crown in the north eastern part of Shimla with a height of 3400 metres. A prominent adventure spot for solo travellers, the peak comes with some of the most stunning views. From dense pine forests to snow capped mountains, there is a lot to watch and admire.

Duration: 1 day
Ideal Visit Time: April-June

3.Chanshal pass


Chanshal Pass (or Chanshal Valley) is a high-altitude mountain pass in Himachal Pradesh, India, connecting the Rohru (Pabbar Valley) area with the remote Dodra Kwar valley, known for stunning Himalayan views

Duration: Two days and one night
Ideal visit time: April - june for snowfall , august - oct for lush green meadows , Remains closed in peak Winters

  • Altitude: Around 3,755 meters (12,320 ft) situated Below Chanshal Peak, the highest in Shimla.
  • Saru Lake (also known as Saru Tal) is a small, sacred high-altitude lake located approximately 8 km from the Chanshal Top, accessible via a trek across scenic meadows and ridges.
