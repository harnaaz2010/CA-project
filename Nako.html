1. Nako Lake


Located in the Pooh sub-division of Kinnaur, Nako Lake is a high-altitude lake situated at 12,014 ft above sea level and forms a part of the Nako Village boundary. This lake is breathtakingly beautiful and offers a tranquil landscape to visitors. Located in Himachal Pradesh, the Nako Lake is nestled amidst willow and poplar trees.



2. Nako Monastery


Nako Monastry is one of the oldest and most important Buddhist monastries in India. Founded in the 11th century, the monastry is known for its ancient murals, statues, and peaceful surroundings. Perched at a high atitude, Nako Monastry reflects the rich cultural heritage of Tibetan Buddhism and attracts both pilgrims and tourists from around the world.
