1. MANALI


Kullu-Manali is one of the highly-recommended places to visit in Himachal Pradesh. If you are on the lookout for fresh air and green mountains amidst the snow-covered ranges, then Kullu-Manali indeed should be at the top of your list. Solang Valley in Manali during snowfall gives you a chance at skiing and many more such adventure sports. While Kullu on the other hand promises you river-rafting, camping and trekking. Visit places in manali

Ideal duration: 5-6 days




2. KINNAUR VALLEY


Kinnaur Valleyis a stunning region in northeastern Himachal Pradesh, bordering Tibet and known for its dramatic mountain landscapes. It is characterised by the confluence of the Himalaya, Zanskar, and Dhauladhar mountain ranges, with the Satluj river carving through it Visit places in kinnaur valley

Ideal duration: 5-6 days
