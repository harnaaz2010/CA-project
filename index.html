1. MANALI


Kullu-Manali is one of the highly-recommended places to visit in Himachal Pradesh. If you are on the lookout for fresh air and green mountains amidst the snow-covered ranges, then Kullu-Manali indeed should be at the top of your list. Solang Valley in Manali during snowfall gives you a chance at skiing and many more such adventure sports. While Kullu on the other hand promises you river-rafting, camping and trekking.

Visit places in manali

Ideal duration: 5-6 days




2. SHIMLA


Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is one of India's most famous hill stations. Known as the "Queen of Hills," it is situated in the Shivalik range of the Himalayas at an altitude of approximately 2,205 meters.

Ideal duration: 2 Days for the city and 3-4 days for the places around it Ideal Visit Time: November-February / April-June

3. KINNAUR VALLEY


Kinnaur Valley is a stunning region in northeastern Himachal Pradesh, bordering Tibet and known for its dramatic mountain landscapes. It is characterised by the confluence of the Himalaya, Zanskar, and Dhauladhar mountain ranges, with the Satluj river carving through it Visit places in kinnaur valley

Ideal duration: 2-3 days




4. SPITI VALLEY


Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh, is a breathtaking high-altitude cold desert in the Himalayas, known as "Little Tibet" for its stark beauty, ancient Buddhist monasteries, unique Tibetan culture, and stunning landscapes, accessible via scenic but challenging road trips, best visited from May to October for pleasant weather and accessibility, offering adventure, spiritual serenity, and incredible stargazing. Visit places in Spiti valley

Ideal duration: 6-7 days

Best Time to Visit : May - Oct




5. TIRTHAN VALLEY


Tirthan Valley is a serene Himalayan destination in Himachal Pradesh's Kullu district, known for its pristine Tirthan River, lush landscapes, and proximity to the Great Himalayan National Park (GHNP), making it ideal for nature lovers, trekkers, and anglers seeking tranquility away from crowds. It's a base for eco-tourism, offering activities like hiking, trout fishing, bird watching, and exploring traditional villages amidst stunning scenery, with limited traffic and a focus on natural beauty. Visit places in Tirthan valley

Ideal duration: 2-3 days




6. PARVATI VALLEY


Parvati Valley is a stunning Himalayan valley in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its dramatic scenery, the Parvati River, ancient villages like Kasol, Tosh, and Kalga, and spiritual sites like Manikaran; it's a major hub for trekkers, backpackers, and nature lovers seeking adventure on trails to Kheerganga and Pin Parvati Pass, offering a mix of natural beauty, serene vibes, and unique cultural experiences. Visit places in Parvati valley

Ideal duration: 3-4 days




7. DALHOUSIE


Dalhousie is a high-altitude hill station in the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh, India, situated at an elevation of approximately 1,970 meters (6,460 ft). Known for its pristine natural beauty and colonial-era charm, it is spread across five hills—Kathlog, Potreyn, Tehra, Bakrota, and Balun—at the western end of the Dhauladhar mountain range. Visit places in Dalhousie

Ideal duration: 2-3 days



8. McLeod Ganj


Mcleodganj, also called "Little Lhasa," is a charming suburb of Dharamshala in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its vibrant Tibetan culture, home to the Dalai Lama and Tibetan government-in-exile, offering spiritual retreats, treks (like Triund), Tibetan handicrafts, and scenic Himalayan views. It's a popular destination for trekking, meditation, and experiencing Tibetan life amidst the Dhauladhar mountains, with pleasant weather from April to June and snowy winters from December to February. Visit places in McLeod Ganj

Ideal duration: 2-3 days
